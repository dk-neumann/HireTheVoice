About Me

Decades of performance. One unforgettable voice.

I'm a versatile voice actor with decades of performance experience, delivering everything from dark, theatrical narration to warm, character-driven storytelling. My range spans documentary authority, educational clarity, and engaging promotional reads.

On the commercial side, I bring high-energy announcer spots, comedic character work, folksy authenticity, and regional accents. Adaptable, credible, and creative — I'm a strong fit for projects spanning audiobooks, documentaries, e-learning, advertising, and character voices.

On the e-learning side, I have a Ph.D. in Education and 20+ years of experience in classroom instruction. I provide clear, engaging narration for educational content.
